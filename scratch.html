Bucket List Hikes

Mt. Timpanogos

Distance: ~14.2 miles round trip

Elevation Gain: ~4,471 feet

Key Highlights: Passes through lush meadows, the alpine forest of Timpooneke Basin, and numerous waterfalls.

Starting Point: Timpooneke Campground off the Alpine Scenic Loop (Highway 92).

Lake Blanche

Distance: ~6.5 to 7.4 miles round-trip

Elevation Gain: ~2,700 to 2,800 feet.

Key Highlights: Beautiful views of Lake Blanche which sits directly beneath the jagged, iconic silhouette of Sundial Peak.

Starting Point: 4.4 miles up Big Cottonwood Canyon at the Mill B South Fork Picnic Ground.

Angels Landing

Distance: ~5.4 miles round-trip

Elevation Gain: ~1,488 feet

Key Highlights: 360-degree bird's-eye view of the Virgin River winding through Zion Canyon

Starting Point: The Grotto Trailhead (Zion Canyon Shuttle Stop #6)
